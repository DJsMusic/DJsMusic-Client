Welcome!

DJs Music is now closed. I run this website from 2007 until 2020, for 13 years of my life.

This was where I first learnt about database optimization, production code, supporting users... it was an amazing ride and an experience that jump-started my professional career later on.

At its peak, DJs Music served over 3Tb of traffic every month, it had arond 20k active users, 7k songs, and millions of visits every single month.

Unfortunately, at some point in the journey Google decided that DJs Music breached the ToS of Adsense and banned the account automatically, citing copyright issues. I had always been very proactive towards copyright claims and removed any songs that received complaints (which only happened about 3 times in those 13 years). After AdSense I tried a few other ad networks but none paid nearly as much, so the website stopped generating revenue.

In any case, I'll leave this here for a little longer, since the domain is still mine. If you want to buy the domain get in touch.

If you are a DJ, and enjoy creating or mixing tunes start uploading it here!
